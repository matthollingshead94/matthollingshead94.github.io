Common questions

What size site do you work with?
Work tends to be most valuable past 10k indexable pages, where small technical decisions start compounding. I'll tell you in our first call whether your project is a fit.
Do you implement changes yourself, or work through our engineering team?
I work through your engineering team. I write specs detailed enough to hand to a developer who isn't an SEO, and I'm available during implementation for questions and review. If you don't have engineering capacity, I can point you to people who do.
How long until I see results?
Crawling and indexation fixes show up in days or weeks. Ranking and traffic improvements depend on what's been holding the site back and how often Google crawls you. I'll give you an honest range during the audit, not a number meant to win the deal.
Do you handle content or link building?
No. I focus on the technical layer: crawling, rendering, indexation, and architecture. Happy to recommend people I trust for the rest.
What does an engagement cost?
Audits start at $5k. Migrations and retainers are scoped per project. I send a fixed quote after a first call.
